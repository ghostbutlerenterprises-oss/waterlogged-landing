How it works

Two seasons of fishing, reconstructed in one tap.

Everyone else makes you log one fish at a time. Waterlogged backfills years of catches from the photos you already took — then turns that history into an edge.

No. 01

Camera-roll backfill

Select your photos and Waterlogged reads the GPS and timestamp from each one, identifies the species, and clusters them into trips. Your whole history appears as a logbook — no manual entry.

The first-run wow
No. 02

Bite forecasting

Because your log starts full, your forecast is personal from day one. “Your reds hit a falling tide, first light, water 72–78°.” A 3-day captain's report tuned to your real catches.

Why you keep it
No. 03

Captain's Log

Run your own boat? Log launches, crew, fuel, and engine hours, with maintenance reminders and a float plan you can text ashore. Every trip's catches and conditions attach automatically.

For boat owners
Why it's different

One fishery, done right.

We're not trying to identify every fish on earth. A focused inshore tool — tuned to a few dozen Tampa Bay species, with FWC slot and bag limits built in — beats a bloated do-everything app for the way you actually fish.

  • Backfill, not data entryImport the past instead of logging one fish at a time. It's the least-copied idea in the space.
  • Your patterns, shown plainlyWe mine your own history and show the “why” behind every call — no opaque bite score.
  • Your spots stay yoursLocation is private by default. Nobody shares honey holes — and we don't make you.
