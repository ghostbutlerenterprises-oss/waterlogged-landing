How it works

Two seasons of fishing, reconstructed in one tap.

Everyone else makes you log one fish at a time. Waterlogged backfills years of catches from the photos you already took — then turns that history into an edge.

Camera-roll backfill

Select your photos and Waterlogged pulls the GPS and timestamp from each one, identifies the species, and clusters them into trips. Your whole history appears as a logbook — no manual entry.

The first-run wow

Bite forecasting

Because your log starts full, your forecast is personal from day one — not generic for months. "Your reds hit a falling tide, first light, water 72–78°." A 3-day captain's report tuned to your real catches.

Why you keep it

Captain's Log

Run your own boat? Log launches, crew, fuel, and engine hours, and get maintenance reminders — with a float plan you can text ashore. Every trip's catches and conditions attach automatically.

Why it's different

One fishery, done right.

We're not trying to identify every fish on earth. A focused inshore tool — tuned to a few dozen Tampa Bay species, with FWC slot and bag limits built in — beats a bloated do-everything app for the way you actually fish.
